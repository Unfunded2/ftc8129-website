Welcome to FTC Team 8129 - Sons of Liberty

We are a FIRST Tech Challenge robotics team from Pilgrim High School in Warwick, Rhode Island. Founded in 2018, our team is dedicated to inspiring students through STEM education, innovation, and competition.

About Our Team

FTC Team 8129 Sons of Liberty is a student-led robotics team at Pilgrim High School. Our mission is to foster creativity, problem-solving, and teamwork through the design and building of robots for the FIRST Tech Challenge.

Our team consists of dedicated students from grades 9-12, mentored by experienced teachers and community volunteers. We participate in regional and state competitions, showcasing our engineering skills and competitive spirit.

At Pilgrim High School, we believe in the power of hands-on learning. Our robotics program integrates science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) into our curriculum, preparing students for future careers in these fields.
